##### Langkah-langkah menyiapkan Pumpkin Cinnamon Rolls:

1. Spread on pumpkin butter and sprinkle on cinnamon and brown sugar
1. Roll up and cut into 7 or 8 equal slices
1. Bake for 18-20 minutes
1. Enjoy!
